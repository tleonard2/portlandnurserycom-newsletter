March 7, 2019
We are beyond excited to announce that Portland Nursery is the Official Garden Center of the 2019 Rose Festival Parade and are the sole carrier of this year’s Rose Festival Rose "Sun Shower"! Both of our locations are fully stocked with this stunning rose as well as a wide array of other varieties. Come in today to make sure you get yours!
The 2019 Rose 'Sun Shower' is a disease-resistant, bushy Floribunda with apricot, yellow and pink fade-resistant petals. Sun Shower is designed to bloom abundantly and continuously all summer. The special shrub by hybridizer William Radler is now available exclusively at Portland Nursery for $29.99 ($2 of the purchase price will go to the Portland Rose Festival Foundation).
