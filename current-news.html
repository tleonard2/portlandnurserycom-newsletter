January is Houseplant month and we get you ready with classes and our Annual Houseplant Sale starting January 10.
Working inside on these cold days? It's a good time to refresh your houseplants and take care of any problems you may be having. January is houseplant month and we offer classes for addressing specific problems.
Then take advantage of the information on our Houseplant Feature Page to make lists of the plants you'd like to pick up at our Annual Houseplant Sale!

If you'd like help with a new garden, come into our Division location and visit our Landscape Planner, Terri. She'll look at what you have in your garden and listen to what you'd like to change. Then she will put together a planting plan for you to work on throughout the spring.
Bring us your Poinsettia carcass in January and we'll give you a $5.00 reward good towards your same day purchase. We don’t care where you bought it, the color it is or the condition it’s in. Dead or Alive you’ll get the REWARD! (One per customer, please.)
