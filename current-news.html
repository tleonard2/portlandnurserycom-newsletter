Pumpkin Decorating Fun
Portland Nursery at 9000 SE Division
Under cover, out of the rain.
Saturday October 29 - 10:00-5:00pm
Face painting and other free activities for kids and the family. Bring your carved or decorated pumpkin to our Division Street store for a chance to win prizes!
No pumpkin? No worries. Come on in cause we're rolling out the orange carpet - our greenhouse is decorated for Halloween fun.
