Passiflora 'Anastasia'
Looking for something rare and unusual for your yard? We have specialty vines and annuals in from multiple growers including Annie's Annuals.
The annuals listed in this newsletter are only a fraction of the rare and unusual plants we have. We only have a flat or two of each plant variety in stock so hurry in to experience the best selection! Our staff will be on hand to explain the care for individual specialty plants.
We have a nice selection of specialty vines including Passiflora ‘Anastasia’, Passiflora mollissima, and Dipogon lignosus.
The plus side of annual vines:
  • Quick vertical cover in one year
  • Flower summer to fall
  • Great for trellising from window boxes or pots
  • Exotic, colorful flowers
  • Can be grown through hedges or other vines for added color
