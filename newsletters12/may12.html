COOKING WITH FRESH HERBS FROM THE GARDEN WITH CHEF DAN BROPHY begins in June.
All Classes are on Wednesday evenings at 5:30 at 5050 SE Stark.
Sign up on our Events Page

This summer, we welcome back Chef Dan Brophy for a new class series on cooking with fresh herbs from the garden. An herb garden can be one of the easiest and most rewarding ways to enhance your cooking with homegrown kitchen staples. Each class will focus on a different seasonal herb or category of herbs, covering cultural tips, harvest, use, and preservation tips, a cooking demonstration and some recipes to take home. Come to one or all of these weeknight classes!.

  • June 13 - Mint in its many varieties with some international flair
  • June 27 - Cilantro and epazote: cooked and raw applications
  • July 11 - Basil and all things basilicious
  • July 25 - Parsley, sage, rosemary and thyme: soups, sauces and more
  • August 8 - Salad herbs: arugula, mustard, sorrel, cress
  • August 22 - Frondy and flavorful: dill and fennel
  • September 5 - Fire from the garden: chiles
