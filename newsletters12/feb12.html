Honeycrisp Apples

Have your own HONEYCRISP apple tree!

Why pay grocery store prices when you can have your own Honeycrisp Apple tree? Pick up one for your garden at Portland Nursery today.

Honeycrisp Apple trees need to be planted near by another apple variety to ensure pollination, and 'Liberty' is an excellent choice. 'Liberty' has firm texture and sweet juicy flavor perfect for eating fresh, cooking and canning. Even better, 'Liberty' is the most disease resistant apple variety we stock.

Vegetable Starts are rolling in!

Spring is in the air and you can get you veggies going! We have our first round of vegetable starts including Broccoli, Cabbage, Chard, Kale, Lettuce, and Spinach. These babies still need tender love and care so plant them in a protected area such as in a cold frame. Also just in is our full selection of potatoes (See our 2012 Potato Table), garlic and most of our onions. If your soil is not too wet it is a great time to get these in along with asparagus! We are also flush with pea starts, edible and flowering sweet peas.
