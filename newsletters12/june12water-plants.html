Saturday, July 7th 1-2:30pm Starting Your Fall & Winter Vegetable Garden
Believe it or not, now is the time to start planning and planting your garden for the fall and winter months! Donna and Robyn, of Your Backyard Farmer, will discuss timing your plantings, good varieties for fall & winter growing, cover crops, and various ways to protect your veggies from the winter weather. Sunday, July 8th 1-2:30pm Troubleshooting in the Vegetable Garden
Is some creature or bug eating your greens? Do you have splotches, spots, powdery coatings, or other worrisome markings on your edibles? Join Jolie Grindstaff, Portland Nursery staff, to learn how to troubleshoot what might be affecting your veggie garden. She will provide great tips and techniques on how to identify and treat common pest, fungus and disease problems in the veggie garden. Healthy crops lead to bountiful harvests!

Saturday, July 14th 10-4:30pm Build Your Own Willow Furniture

Saturday, July 21st 1-2:30pm Controlling Pests/Diseases in the Landscape

About the Instructors
Gary and Nancy Leahy recently moved to Portland from Wisconsin to be closer to family. They have been farmers for most of their lives, and their passion for home canning began decades ago with a desire to feed their 3 young boys a healthier diet. In 2009, they both became certified for Wisconsin Acidified Canned Foods Training, which includes the microbiology of canned foods, principles of canning, food safety and sanitation, and facilities requirements. They went home, started canning like crazy, and have been doing so ever since! They have canned everything from peaches to beets, salsas, relishes, meats, pickled veggies, sauces and juices. Nancy just graduated from the Institute for Integrative Nutrition in May, and Gary will be graduating in March of 2013. They are both passionate about healthy eating as well as sharing their knowledge and excitement with others.
