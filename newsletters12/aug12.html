We've Got Apples! Special 30% Off for Subscribers

Our apple growers picked this week and surprised us with a delivery of Gravenstein and Early Gold apples. We're selling these as a pre-Apple Tasting special for the insanely low price of $0.79 per pound.

BRING IN THIS COUPON ONLY OFFERED TO OUR NEWSLETTER SUBSCRIBERS FOR AN ADDITIONAL 30% OFF!

Gravenstein: Hands down the most requested apple that we're unable to offer at Apple Tasting. This is the best of the early-Summer apples! Tangy, rich, tender, crisp and juicy, it is the perfect antidote to the bland flavor of many of the August apples. Gravensteins makes exceptional pies! Although a poor-keeper, it will last several weeks if refrigerated. Don't miss this chance to taste a treasured heirloom! When they're gone, they're gone…

Early Gold: Another flavorful, early apple, the Early Gold is rather similar to the Ginger Gold (the apple we use in our apple streudel) but has more tartness. Early Gold's fine grain structure makes a wonderful, smooth apple sauce.
