Help the Oregon Food Bank put food on kids' plates this Spring Break!

This week is Spring Break, a welcome treat for most kids. But for the many students reliant on school breakfasts and/or lunches for their meals Spring Break is a lot bleaker.

Portland Nursery supports the Oregon Food Bank and you can too! Help us keep food on kids' plates by contributing to our food drive.

Bring non-perishable, packaged, low-fat and low-sugar foods to either Portland Nursery location, through March 31st and we'll give you:

  • A FREE packet of seeds for your garden
  • Entry in a raffle for a $50 Portland Nursery Gift Card
  • The satisfaction of knowing you're making a difference for kids in need
