How it works (Stark street only)
  1. Read about these three great local gardening nonprofits who would benefit from your support.
  2. Select an ornament from the Giving Tree that reflects the amount you'd like to donate.
  3. Bring the ornament to the cashier to buy a Portland Nursery Gift Card for the chosen organization.
We will send the gift card. and for your generosity, we'll give you a coupon for 10% off your NEXT purchase. One coupon per purchase. Coupon can be applied at either location!
