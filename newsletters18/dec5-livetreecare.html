Caring For Your Live Holiday Tree
  • Live trees can only be indoors for 7 days.
  • The tree must be acclimated to indoor temperatures before being brought inside.
  • Place your tree in a garage or protected porch for 3-5 days before bringing it indoors.
  • Once inside keep the tree away from heating vents, very sunny windows, wood stoves, etc.
  • If you plan on using lights, use only small, cool burning bulbs.
  • Watering your tree is very important. Ice cubes on the soil are a great way to slowly water the tree and keep the root ball cool.
  • Remember to transition your tree to outdoor temperatures before placing it back outside. (Put it back in the garage or protected porch for another 5-7 days.)
  • If you plan on using it for several years it may be best to keep it in a sheltered spot away from strong winds and freezing temperatures.
For more information, download a “Live Holiday Trees” brochure or pick one up from our information Desk.
