Pilgrim Imports - only at 5050 SE Stark!
Pilgrim Imports originated almost 30 yeas ago in a village outside of Chiang Mai, Thailand. Three decades ago this was a chronically poor area. Families accustomed to closeness were commonly forced to separate for survival as children were sent to work in Bangkok's factories and tourist trades. Traditional village life deteriorated in the face of poverty.
No longer do the villagers consider sending their kids away to the big city. Now over 400 neighbors in this rural land work together to produce the art pieces Pilgrim customers love. Utilizing their traditional metalworking skills that are able to remain in their homeland.
There are many tasks that need to be completed to create Pilgrim’s adorable ornaments. Fortunately, all of theses jobs are hones, clean, and economical. No one thinks of dumping trash in river or landfills because the villagers work right where they live. No lead or other heavy metals are used in their production.
More importantly, these artisans are proud to preserve their cultural traditions and happy to be close to each other. They appreciate the work which sustains their way of life, and are pleased to know the beauty they create is enjoyed around the world.
