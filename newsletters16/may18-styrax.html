Styrax japanonica
Nature chimes in May with Styrax
Perfect white bell-shape flowers with light fragrance nod gracefully along the branches in May. Dark green leaves about 2” long turn yellow before dropping in autumn. Decorative green fruit follows flowers into summer. The overall shape is upright and pyramidal with horizontal side branches, making a nice canopy.

Just for entering, you'll get a 10% off coupon (one per customer). All entries are judged by the public and the staff. Awards for 3 staff voted winners and 3 customer voted winners include:
  • Two - First Place Winners each receive $100 Gift card
  • Two - Second Place Winners each receive $50 Gift card
  • Two - Third Place Winners each receive $25 Gift card
  • All Entrants receive a 10% off coupon (one per customer) good at any Portland Nursery.
