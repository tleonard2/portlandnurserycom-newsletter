Chef Dan Brophy (Chef-Instructor at Oregon Culinary Institute) has taught several series cooking workshops here at Portland Nursery. We've pulled together the recipes from his Food Preservation series, his Cooking with Herbs series and his Apple Tasting Event series.
We have two pages dedicated to his recipes with pdf versions for downloading. Here are just a few to get you started.

  • Apple Compote
