February 6, 2020
There is a lot of fresh, new plant material coming in throughout the month, so get ready to liven up those porch containers and, if your soil is workable, your garden beds as well. Add primroses around a walk, plant some seeds indoors, get some structure in the garden with miniature evergreens, add cyclamen indoors and out. Missed bulb planting? We have potted crocus, hyacinths, and daffodils for color spot additions.
Are you interested of reducing water usage in your own garden? A well thought-out design helps you get water-smart plantings that are also beautiful! a great time to think about Garden Design and our Custom Planting Plans make it easy!
Our landscape designer, Terrie Burdette, can help you plan for a future of less watering and more curb appeal! Make an appointment and get inspired! Bring Terrie your garden dimensions and photos and she'll create a design and plant list that will transform any garden space!
A custom design will help make your garden a success. See our Custom Planting Plans Page for all the information you need to get started.
