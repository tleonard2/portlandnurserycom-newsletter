March 14, 2020
A Note from Portland Nursery
Like so many of you, we have spent the last several weeks learning about the coronavirus (COVID-19) and how it is impacting the world and us locally here in the Pacific Northwest. We understand the growing concern surrounding the unknowns and we want you to know that your health and safety is our top priority. In order to keep you and our team safe, we are following the Centers for Disease Control (CDC) guidelines to help prevent the spread of the virus (see below).
In addition to our normal cleaning schedule, we have encouraged our team to frequently disinfect their works stations and high-touch areas at our stores.
There are currently no plans for closure of stores, however, we will share any service interruptions that affect our customers on our website. For the most up-to-date information please follow us on Facebook and Instagram.
This may be a gift of time. Slow down, smell the roses, and reconnect with the simple pleasures of being at home. We are committed to making it easy for you to get the product you love. We want to help our customers with the resiliency a garden can offer for food and personal enjoyment.
As always, we’re grateful for the opportunity to serve you. Rest assured, we’re doing everything in our power to provide a safe shopping experience for our customers and a healthy environment for our staff.
-Portland Nursery
CDC recommendations include:
  • Wash your hands frequently, using soap and water for at least 20 seconds, or using an alcohol-based sanitizer. Both of our locations have these hand washing supplies on hand.
  • Avoid touching your face – eyes, nose and mouth.
  • Cover coughs and sneezes with a tissue and throw away the tissue.
  • Avoid close contact with people who are sick.
  • Stay home if you are sick or have flu-like symptoms (fever, cough, shortness of breath).
  • For more information, visit CDS Coronavirus (COVID-19) Webpage
