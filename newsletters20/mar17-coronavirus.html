March 17, 2020
Portland Nursery will be temporarily closed starting tomorrow, Wednesday, March 18, due to the current State of Emergency.
We will be re-evaluating this decision continually until we feel there’s enough information to open in a safe manner that protects our staff, customers and their families.
We look forward to helping you with all of your gardening needs upon reopening.
Stay resilient Portland. ❤️
Sincerely,
The Portland Nursery Family
All classes have been canceled. We will have new classes listed upon reopening.
