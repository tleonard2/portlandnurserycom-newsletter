Hydrangeas make a wonderful patio or deck plant. Their hardiness keeps them blooming year after year. Potted up, they become a colorful addition to your outdoor living space and care is easy. Get a summer bloomer like "Endless Summer" and you'll have gorgeous blooms all summer long!
Their spent blooms make for winter interest. In the spring, cut off the spent blooms, prune out a few stems of old wood and watch it fill out and bloom again!
We have a wealth of information on our Hydrangea Page. You'll find varieties, pruning tips and even how to change a hydrangea's colors!
