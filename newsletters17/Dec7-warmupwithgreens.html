Don't let these gray days get you down! Bring the green inside! We have greens galore and all the extras you need to decorate for the season.
Our feature this month is Holiday Cheer Baskets.
You'll get a few tips and photos of some of the great greens we have in stock!
Gather your Supplies
1) Hanging baskets. Those left over from summer are fine. We tend to use iron baskets with coir fiber linings.
2) Potting soil.
3) Christmas greens, cut floral items, and any other decorations you would like to accent your basket with.
A good design principle is to add the most structural and colorful items in odd numbers. Trim off leaves, needles, and branches from the base of your material so that it’s easier to position in the soil.
Additional Options
Other Christmas greens we carry include Holly, Juniper with berries, Western Red Cedar, Princess Pine, Noble Fir, and Incense Cedar.
Add color to your basket with Hypericum fruits, Leucadendron, variegated Boxwood, and Evergreen Huckleberry.
Cut floral items we carry include Protea, Eucalyptus pods and stems, mums, Alstroemeria, Ilex berry, Statice, and Sea Holly.
Finally, add some finishing touches with rose hips, dyed reindeer moss, Spanish moss, pine cones, birds, Eucalyptus pods, or miniature decorative apples and pears.
