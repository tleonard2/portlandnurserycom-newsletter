May is a good time to be planting your containers. Building them with a combination of plants is a challenge and one that is a pleasure for every gardener. Use the simple formula of combining a 'thriller' with a 'spiller' and a 'filler.'
Start with a “thriller,” a centerpiece plant with star quality, something big, bold, and beautiful such as the Lobelia laxiflora shown above. Surround it with a filler, for example, a marigold and then fill the remainder with a spiller such as Calibrachoa ‘Cabaret Orange’ or Nasturtium ‘Empress of India’. (See the plant locations in our May Container Recipe.
Use each of those kinds of plants—in various proportions—and take care to balance colors and textures. We have selections of a rainbow of colors in each.
A basic recipe for a fantastic container is described on our Thriller, Spiller, Filler Page.
