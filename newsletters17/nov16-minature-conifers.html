Miniature conifers offer colors, textures, shapes, and sizes perfect for any container. Since they add visual interest throughout the year, they're excellent plants to use as accents and as components of major plantings. "Dwarf or Miniature" is a loosely defined word, so think of them as plants that grow slowly and act as shrubs rather than as the tree species from which most are selected.
The conifers come in a wide range of colors and textures, and can play off of their surroundings in beautiful and unique ways. Our feature on Miniature Conifers provides you with more information on selection and growth rates.
