Community Update
All year long, Portland Nursery reaches out to the community with donations to over 400 local schools and non-profits. In addition, our staff finds time for involvement in a variety of community gardens. This month Portland Nursery staff participated in the Timbers Stand Together Project and the Zenger Farms Urban Grange Hall ribbon cutting. We hope that features like these will introduce you to all the wonderful gardening projects in our great city and their contribution to our community.
The Timbers Stand Together Project

Portland Nursery staff participated in the Timbers Stand Together project planting sunflowers, squash and tomatoes at the Hilltop Community Garden which contributes to Tualatin Food Pantry (under the umbrella of The Oregon Food Bank).

The Portland Timbers Stand Together Week is a community initiative supporting dozens of nonprofit projects and volunteer opportunities across the city.
Portland Nursery is a proud supporter of Zenger Farm!

We were very excited to be a part of the ribbon-cutting and grand opening celebration of Zenger Farm’s brand-new Urban Grange! Zenger Farm is a 4-acre working farm in Portland. Their mission is to “create experiential and science-based programs that teach youth and adults the importance of food, farming, wetland conservation and environmental stewardship in healthy urban communities.”

The Urban Grange, with its new classroom and kitchen facilities, will allow Zenger to double the amount of visitors and educational opportunities they provide at the farm each year, through their many workshops, Farm Camp, and field trips. Portland Nursery is delighted to be a part of Zenger Farm’s growing future.
