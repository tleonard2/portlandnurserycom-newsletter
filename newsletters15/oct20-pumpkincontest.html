Calling all pumpkin carvers and decorators!

Portland Nursery is proud to host our first Pumpkin Carving Contest. Bring your kids and your carved or decorated pumpkins to our Division Street store for a chance to win prizes!

Don’t like carving pumpkins? Come admire all the creative work of others in our neighborhood! Saturday and Sunday we’ll have face painting and other free activities for kids and the family, as well as kids and adult category prizes!
