Anatomy of a Container Design

Just for fun, we thought we'd take apart this month's container design to show you how things go together. Each month, one of our staff takes on the challenge of creating a seasonal container using plants available in the nursery. We give you the 'recipe' so you can put it together. If it looks too daunting, we'd be happy to do it for you! We hope you enjoy this month's Blush of Snow.
  1. Viburnum tinus ‘Compacta’
  2. Carex buchananii: Leatherleaf Sedge
  3. Sedum tetractinum: Chinese Sedum
  4. Cheilanthes lanosa: Hairy Lip Fern
  5. Cyclamen persicum ‘Mini White’
  6. Heuchera ‘Crimson Curls’
  7. Astelia nervosa ‘Westland’
  8. Cyclamen coum ‘Silver Form’
  9. Holiday accents: pinecones and Huckleberry branches
Color inside the house!
Amaryllis and Paperwhite bulbs make great gifts! Wrap them up with our handy handout on forcing bulbs!

We also have beautiful blooming Christmas Cactus, Orchids, Poinsettias in red, white and the in-between pink! And when you visit us, don't forget, our remaining spring blooming bulbs are still on sale for 50% off!

Looking for a great way to give back to the community? We're hosting a Holiday Giving Tree, with 3 local nonprofit organizations benefiting from your generosity: Bradley Angle, Leach Botanical Garden and Outgrowing Hunger!

How can you help? All you have to do is buy a gift card for any amount at either store location, and we'll donate it directly to whichever group you choose. The groups will be able to make purchases for their gardening programs with the donated gift cards, plus Portland Nursery will extend to them an additional discount when they come shopping, so your dollar goes even further.

You get the satisfaction of giving back, and, for your generosity, we'll give YOU a coupon good for $5 off your next purchase of $20 or more! You can read all the details on this Giving Tree PDF

Please visit the individual nonprofit websites to find out about the great work they do in our community:

  • Bradley Angle
  • Leach Botanical Garden
  • Outgrowing Hunger
