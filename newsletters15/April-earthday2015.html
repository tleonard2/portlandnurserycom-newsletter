Where every drop of water counts!
Take time to honor the earth this month

As gardeners, we are aware of the need to take care of the environment that feeds us, body and soul. Planting trees, growing our own veggies, connecting with our family and paying attention to our global family is a thread we think most people embrace.

Water conservation is in the news every day. In the middle of Pacific Northwest rain, we sometimes forget how important it is to us as a global community. It is a topic that effects our pocket books, our earth and our neighbors. We want to take this episode of the newsletter to pass on a few things that can help.
How can we help?

Are you interested of reducing water usage in your own garden? A well thought-out design helps you get water-smart plantings that are also beautiful!

Our landscape designer, Terrie Burdette, can help you plan for a future of less watering and more curb appeal! Visit our Landscape Design page for more information.
