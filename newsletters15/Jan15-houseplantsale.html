Make this the year
for Mason Bees!

You too can become a bee keeper with mason bees! It’s easy to get started with this native pollinator. The orchard mason bee (Osmia lignaria) is a small solitary bee which lives in thin reeds or holes left behind by other insects. They are typically non‐stinging and since they don’t live in swarms these bees are easy to keep as “pets”, providing excellent pollinating services in early spring. Read the whole article...>>

We have everything you need to get started. Pick out a bee home for your new friends. Combine it with our Bee House starter kits and the bees to take up residence.
