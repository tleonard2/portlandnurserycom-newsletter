Update your pots with miniature conifers!

These little gems offer colors, textures, shapes, and sizes perfect for any container. Since they add visual interest throughout the year, they're excellent plants to use as accents and as components of major plantings. "Dwarf or Miniature" is a loosely defined word, so think of them as plants that grow slowly and act as shrubs rather than as the tree species from which most are selected.

The conifers come in a wide range of colors and textures, and can play off of their surroundings in beautiful and unique ways. Our feature on Miniature Conifers provides you with more information on selection and growth rates.

This month's container design features Chamaecyparis ‘Nana Lutea’ (False Cyprus) and Abies pinsapo ‘Horstmann’ (Spanish Fir) combined with Sorrel and Viola.

Health Benefits of
Urban Gardening

Dr. Daniel DeLapp, backyard organic food gardener for over 50 years, will share his insights on the expected and unexpected health benefits of having a backyard garden all year long. Backyard gardens have the potential to: boost our immune system, optimizing nutrient intake, improve gut health and enhancing our mental and physical well-being. It also has positive effect for our environment by reducing pesticide exposure and improving the soil microbiome. Come share your enthusiasm for gardening, whether you are new to gardening or an experienced gardener already.
