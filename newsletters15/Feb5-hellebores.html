Experience the Winter Jewels!
Many people struggle with what to plant in shady areas, especially finding evergreen and colorful plants that will tolerate shade. Hellebores are the perfect solution. They love the shade and have colorful blooms that last and last! Hellebores are low-maintenance and hardy making them a great addition to any garden. But our favorite part about Hellebores is that they bloom when almost everything else is just trying to make it through winter, including us. They're guaranteed to brighten up those overcast Portland days.
Read more below about these spring 'jewels.'

The Winter Jewel Series
From humble beginnings in 1992, Northwest Garden Nursery has become the major breeder of hellebores here in the Pacific Northwest. Their original breeding stock came as seed from breeders Will McLewin in the U.K. and Gisela Schmeimann in Germany. The Gold Collection
The Gold Collection of Hellebores offers a natural progression of blooms from November through March. Selected by Heuger Trading in Europe, these hellebores make wonderful hostess gifts, and dress up any container or garden bed. Many have been selected for outward facing, cream flowers with accents of green or antique rose. Read more..
