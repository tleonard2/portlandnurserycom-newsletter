Schedule Your Own Wreath Making Party!!
Make it a yearly tradition!

Gather with friends and family to handmake decor for your home or to gift to another. We will provide the materials, space and instruction - you bring a group between 5-10, gloves and pruners and leave the clean-up to us. Plan to spend 1 ½ hours in the workshop. New this year, your group can choose to make either wreaths or kissing balls! Please let us know which you would like so we have the right material.
All you need to do is give us a call and tell us the date you want!
  • Cost: $30 per project (select either wreaths or kissing balls).
  • Please bring gloves and pruners if you have them.
  • Limited to 12 students.
Pick your store and Party Dates:
  • December 1-20 between 9:30 – 3:00pm.

We truly have everything you'll need. We have gifts for the whole family and it's a great time to take advantage of Little Boxes Friday and Small Business Saturday and enter to win great prizes.

Little Boxes is a city-wide shopping event and prize raffle you can enter by going to any participant store. Prizes include a travel package, an iPad, shopping sprees, hotel packages, restaurant gift certificates and TONS more goodies!

When you make a purchase at one Little Box, show your passport or iPhone to the next Little Box and you’ll unlock a 10% discount*.

In Addition, with each purchase you earn bonus raffle entries. Collect four and you get 15 extra entries in the raffle. Eight stamps and you get another 20 extra entries. 12 or more stamps and you get an additional 30 raffle entries!
