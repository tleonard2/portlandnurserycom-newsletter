February 8, 2016

Spring at Portland Nursery is fast-paced and intensely busy. We are looking for people who can multitask like crazy and who are undaunted, if not downright inspired, by an atmosphere of high energy and demands in their workplace. We are looking for individuals who are willing and able to work as part of a team; we need a staff who can work together and stay calm, cheerful and enthusiastic through it all.
Applicants having plant knowledge is definitely a plus, but successful customer service and cashiering experience are of primary importance.
We have openings in one or more of the following Seasonal Positions:
  • Cashier/Customer Service
  • Production (off-loading, potting and stocking plants)
  • Loading Zone/Carry Out
  • Lead Barista (inquire for more detail)
Duties can include:
  • Cashiering
  • Answering multi-line phone and directing calls
  • Customer assistance in locating/selecting plant material, supplies, problem-solving
  • Maintenance of plant material and displays
Necessary Skills:
  • Ability to work well under pressure
  • Ability to work cooperatively
  • Excellent attention to detail
  • Good communication skills
  • Ability to lift and carry fifty (50) pounds
  • Ability to stand for 2-3 hours at a time
If this sounds like you, come to the nursery and fill out an application – or download and fill out the application form linked here, and fax, mail or deliver to us (see addresses in sidebar).
Note: Our Division and Stark locations interview and hire separately. If you are interested in both locations, you must submit a separate application to each.
