WEATHER ALERT

Looks like cold temperatures and snow are on the way to Portland and other points surrounding. If you have planted anything recently and there is some new growth, we recommend using some type of frost protection. Lettuce, broccoli & onion seedlings are all prone to damage as are Daphne and other shrubs that may have emerging leaves.

 

Use row cover or frost blanket to keep frost off newly planted veggie beds. Wrap shrubs with frost blanket to protect new leaves. Cut the tops off plastic bottles or milk jugs and turn them upside down over new starts.

 

Root crops and bulbs should be just fine, and established shrubs and perennials should also weather well. If you want to add a layer of mulch, go for it.
