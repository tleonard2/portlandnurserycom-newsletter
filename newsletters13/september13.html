Annual AppleTasting Event at 5050 SE Stark

Portland Nursery at 5050 SE Stark
Friday-Sunday October 11 - 13
Friday-Sunday October 18 - 20
10:00am - 5:00pm

Our 26th annual Apple Tasting is just around the corner. Seems odd to think about fall, when we’re all still tending our tomatoes and eating fresh peaches. But all too soon the leaves on the trees will begin to change color, and we will be celebrating the abundance of apples and pears.

This has been a great year for fruit, so we expect to be well-supplied with a large variety of apples and pears to taste and purchase by the pound, as many as 60 different varieties in all! The tasting lines will be open during all six days of this free event, and likewise our fresh-pressed cider demonstration and tasting.
