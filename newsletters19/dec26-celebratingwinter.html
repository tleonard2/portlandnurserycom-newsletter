Start a new tradition by gathering with friends and family to hand-make wreaths for your home or to give to another. We will provide the materials, space, and instruction. Bring a group of friends or family, gloves, and pruners and leave the clean-up to us. Our enthusiastic teachers will guide you through the process of making a wreath that is unique to you and guaranteed to bring holiday cheer into your home!
The $35 fee covers basic materials for a mixed green wreath with pinecones and handmade bow. Lots of extra special embellishments to personalize your wreath will be available to purchase additionally.
Dress warmly and bring gloves and hand pruners.
