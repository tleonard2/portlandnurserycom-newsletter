Just in and in bloom - $16.99. Amarine tubergenii belladiva ‘Anastasia’ is a cross between Amaryllis and Nerine. Blooms September through November, so makes a great fall color addition.
The bright pink plant likes full sun and reaches a height of 13-24 inches. Space 8-12 inches and let dry between waterings. Hearty to Zone 7. Perennial. Clumping habit.
