Last week we mistakenly featured a 'Thanksgiving Cactus' in our article titled 'Christmas Cactus'. While the care is similar for all three, we wanted to let you know (thanks to a reader!) that we apologize for the error.
The Thanksgiving cactus has pointy side leaves and the Christmas cactus has more rounded ones. They both have about the same day length requirements for blooming and have been crossed so much that it is sometimes hard to tell which is which.
The third type – Easter cactus, with leaves more similar to the Christmas cactus, has slightly different light requirements. Refer to this brief article from the Iowa State Extension service: Is it a Thanksgiving, Christmas or Easter Cactus?
