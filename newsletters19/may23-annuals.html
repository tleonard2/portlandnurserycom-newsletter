May 23, 2019
During the spring months, we welcome back our full annual selection. We have in the cooler season bedding plants such as Alyssum and Snapdragons, the full spectrum of Geraniums and Fuchsias plus a huge selection of specialty annuals. Our specialty annuals are organized into thriller, filler, and spiller sections to help make container design fun and easy.
Suggestions for your sun baskets:
  • Petunias
  • Marigolds
  • Osteospermum
  • Gazania
  • Gerbera
  • Lantana
Print out the full list of recommended annuals for Sun Baskets.
Suggestions for your shade baskets:
  • Coleus
  • Potato Vine
  • Alocasia
  • Begonia Dragon wing
  • Caladium
Print out the full list of recommended annuals for Shade Baskets.
