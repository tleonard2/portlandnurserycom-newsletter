Geraniums are fantastic, brightly colored blooms and foliage that thrive in a wide range of situations. They do best in full sun to partial shade, and bloom all summer when spent blooms are removed.
Geranium combine beautifully with other annuals such as Coleus, Ranunculus and Calibrachoa.
