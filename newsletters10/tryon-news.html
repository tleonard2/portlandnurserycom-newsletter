Environmental Book Drive!

Did you know that there is only 1 book for every 300 children in low income neighborhoods? 

This mind boggling paucity of books presents one of the biggest impediments to literacy. 

Please help the Children’s Book Bank by bringing in any hardcover, soft cover, board books and beginner’s chapter books you may have lying around. 

We would especially appreciate environmentally-oriented children’s books, but any books will provide remarkable assistance. We will have a drop off basket behind the front desk in the visitor’s center. If you need any assistance in locating the basket please do not hesitate to ask. 

Thank you for your help!


Trillium Festival April 10-11

Spring is near and we are ready to welcome it with our Annual Trillium Festival!

Each year the Friends of Tryon Creek State Park and Oregon Parks and Recreation Department are thrilled to share the beauty of our awakening forest with visitors as they enjoy this wonderful community event.

Enjoy our Nature & Hardy Plant Sale, Kids Activities, Guided Nature Walks, Community Partners. Spring Forest Market and more.

Visit our Trillium Festival webpage for details.
