Bloodworks NW is Portland’s local blood center. We differ from other blood service organizations in that we exclusively collect blood in the communities we serve. We’ve been operating in the Northwest for over 70 years, including over 22 years in the Portland/Vancouver area. We are the sole supplier of blood to all Providence and Legacy hospitals in Oregon, as well as to all Cowlitz, Clark, and Lewis County hospitals in Washington.
