NOTE: Unfortunately, in this week's newsletter, a photo of Yellow Flag Iris was featured with the caption "Iris versicolor." Yellow Flag Iris is an invasive species and is not sold at Portland Nursery. In the online version of the newsletter, the image has been replaced.
If you would like to know more about Yellow Flag Iris, please refer to this article by Oregon State University: Gorgeous yellow iris is ecological threat to PNW wetlands.
