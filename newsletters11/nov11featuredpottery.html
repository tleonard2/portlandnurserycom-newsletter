Sustainably Grown Cut Trees

FRESH CUT TREES ONLY AT 9000 SE DIVISION

The first arrival of fresh cut Noble Fir trees will be the Wednesday before Thanksgiving. The SERF trees will be in around December 1. Call our Division location at 503-788-9000 to confirm.

SERF stands for Socially & Environmentally Responsible Farm. The trees are sustainable and a renewable resource, and can be mulched or composted so no waste goes to landfills. There is no pesticide, fungicide or herbicide used in the growing and harvesting of the trees, and they are only lightly trimmed.
