In the Nursery

WE ARE WELL-STOCKED WITH GRASSES!

This is a great time of year to add grasses to your garden. Our selection is well stocked and we're on hand to help you select the best one for your garden or pot.

Use this List of Grasses to identify which grass fits best. Our Grasses and Bamboo page also has tips for planting and controlling Bamboo.

See these features on grasses and bamboo:

  • Ophiopogon (Mondo Grass)
  • Pennisetum (Fountain Grass)
  • Carex (Sedge)
  • Phyllostachys (Bamboo)

 

Drought Tolerant Gardening

DROUGHT TOLERANT PLANTINGS

This is the time of year that we all start wondering why we didn't plant more drought tolerant plants! Print out our list of Drought Tolerant Plants.

Check out these features on these drought-tolerant perennials:

  • Agastache (Giant Hyssop)
  • Arcostaphylos uva-ursi (Kinnikinnick)
  • Coreopsis (Tickseed)
  • Dianthus (Carnation)
  • Epimedium (Bishop's Hat)
  • Iberis (Candytuft)
  • Kniphofia (Red Hot Poker)
  • Perovskia atriplicifolia (Russian Sage)
  • Thymus spp. (Thyme)

Drought tolerant shrub and tree features:

  • Arbutus menziesii (Madrone)
  • Cotinus (Smoke Tree)
  • Lagerstroemia indica (Crape Myrtle)
  • Punica granatum (Flowering pomegranate)

Plant Picks of the Month

PERENNIAL PICK - Eupatorium/Eutrochium - Joe-Pye Weed
According to Allan Armitage "Joe Pye is said to have been a North Carolina Indian who used these plants to cure many ailments, including typhoid fever and the plants become known as Joe Pye's weed." Read more...

SHRUB PICK - Punica - Pomegranate
Pomegranates have been in cultivation so long that no one knows exactly where they came from. It has been in use for eons in Iran and India and is featured in Egyptian mythology. Read more...

NATIVE PICK - Euriophyllum - Oregon Sunshine
Since this year we are finding big sunny warm days being somewhat elusive here in the Willamette Valley, it seemed like a good time to feature a cheery native perennial whose common name is Oregon sunshine! Eriophyllum lanatum is an easy-going native perennial, bothered by almost nothing except too much water. Want something for that sunny spot beyond the reach of your irrigation? This might be just what you need. Read more...
