Elder Friendly Gardening
with Nels Olson

Your life can be happier and healthier if you continue to be outdoors and work in your garden. Don’t let aches and pains end your favorite pastime.

Nels Olson, registered horticultural therapist, Master Gardener, and Portland Nursery Employee, will share ways of adapting your tools, methods, and plant selections in order to conserve energy, stay safe, and garden smarter.

Seed Saving at Home
with Corina Reynolds-Stearns

Make your plant habit more self-sustaining and help preserve heirloom varieties! Corina will share the basics of seed-saving for veggies, herbs, and flowers.

The class will cover important issues of cross-pollination and crop isolation, various seed-saving techniques, and how to properly store seeds for maximum viability.
