Plan-your-own Wreath Party

We can't say enough about this approach to holiday fun! We started it last year and it was a big success - for us and for you, the party planners!

Gather friends and family to hand-make wreaths for your home or to gift to another. We will provide the materials, space and instruction – you bring a group between 5-10 and leave the clean-up to us.

Materials fee per person: $30 payable to our cashier before class.

Please bring gloves and pruners if you have them. Limited to 12 students.
