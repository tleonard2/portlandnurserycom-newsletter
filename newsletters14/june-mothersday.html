Great Color in the Nursery
Some last minute suggestions for Mother's Day

Our yards are blooming! We did a walk-through and found some plants we think would please any mom. Syringa ‘Bloomerang‘ is a gorgeous lilac! We think Gardenias beat cut flowers any day - they are fragrant and keep blooming all summer! Roses are in bloom now too, so it’s a great time to find your favorite!

We’ve gone overboard with hanging baskets this year. There are easy-care begonia baskets, fuchsia baskets and special gourmet mixed baskets.

If you'd like to give mom a GIFT CARD, give us a call and we can have it ready for you to pick up! Call us at 5050 SE Stark: 503-231-5050 or 9000 SE Division: 503-788-9000. (We will also mail gift cards.)
